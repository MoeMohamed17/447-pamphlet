Know the signs of TB
Tuberculosis (TB) is serious but fully curable. Many people don't know they have it, which is why early testing matters. If you or a family member have any of these symptoms for 2 weeks or more, get tested today.
🫁
Persistent Cough
Lasting 2+ weeks
🌡️
Fever & Night Sweats
Low-grade fever, sweating at night
⚖️
Unexplained Weight Loss
Losing weight without trying
😮‍💨
Fatigue & Weakness
Tired even after a full night's sleep
You don't need symptoms to be tested. TB can be present before you feel unwell. Getting tested now protects you and everyone you love.

Let's clear things up
TB Myths vs. Facts
✗ Myth
"Only poor or weak people get TB."
✓ Fact
TB is a bacterial infection, anyone can get it. Actor Amitabh Bachchan was diagnosed with TB and made a full recovery after treatment.
✗ Myth
"TB testing is expensive and far away."
✓ Fact
Free screening is in your neighbourhood. No hospital trip, just walk to the orange tent.
✗ Myth
"If I test positive, everyone will find out."
✓ Fact
Results are strictly confidential. NTEP contacts only you, privately. You decide what to share.
A public message
"TB is a disease. It can be treated. Don't hide it — fight it."
— Inspired by Amitabh Bachchan, TB survivor & public advocate
Why testing matters
55
Average days between symptoms & treatment in India
80%
Treatment success rate when TB is caught early
Every day without a diagnosis means TB can spread to those around you. The sooner you test, the sooner you protect the people you love.
Based on India's National TB Prevalence Survey 2019–2021 and WHO Global TB Report 2024.
